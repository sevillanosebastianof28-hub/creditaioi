12 CFR Part 1005 - Electronic Fund Transfers (Regulation E) Regulation E protects consumers when they use electronic fund and remittance transfers. Electronic fund and remittance transfers include: ATMs - Direct Deposit - Gift cards - Overdraft - Point of sale transfers - Remittances (international money transfers) - Telephone transfers - The regulation covers topics such as: Disclosure of fees and limits - Cancellation and error correction resolution procedures - Liability - Preauthorized transfers - Receipts -
