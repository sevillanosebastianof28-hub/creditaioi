12 CFR Part 1011 - Land Registration (Regulation K) Regulation K governs purchasers’ revocation rights, describes certain unlawful sales practices, and provides standards and advertising disclaimers under the Interstate Land Sales Full Disclosure Act. Persons subject to the regulation include: Developers - Agents of developers - The regulation covers topics such as: Purchasers’ revocation rights - Sales practices and standards - Advertising disclaimers - Misrepresentation and fraud - Additional resources 2015 Bulletin on Interstate Land Sales Full Disclosure Act amendment 2011 Bulletin on communications with CFPB Press release on a May 2015 Enforcement action Guidelines for Exemptions Available Under the Interstate Land Sales Full Disclosure Act
