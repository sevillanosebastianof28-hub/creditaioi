Advisory Opinion program The Bureau’s Advisory Opinion program provides written guidance to assist regulated entities to better understand their legal and regulatory obligations through advisory opinions. Parties may submit requests for advisory opinions regarding any issue under the Bureau’s purview that can be resolved through an interpretive rule. General regulatory implementation Sign up to get email updates on regulatory implementation. Purpose of the program The Advisory Opinion program provides a mechanism through which the Bureau can carry out its statutory purposes and objectives by better enabling compliance.
