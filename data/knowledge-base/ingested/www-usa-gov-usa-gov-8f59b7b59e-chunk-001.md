Chat with USAGov Chat live with a USAGov agent about federal programs, services, benefits, and more. They will answer your questions or direct you to the government agency that can. Hours of operation USA.gov offers chat service Monday - Friday, 8:00 AM - 8:00 PM Eastern Time (except federal holidays). Troubleshooting tips You may need to disable pop-up blockers to take advantage of all aspects of the chat service. - If you are using Internet Explorer version 11 and cannot start a chat, please try a different browser. - You can call instead at 1-844-USA-GOV1 (1-844-872-4681). - LAST UPDATED: November 12, 2025
