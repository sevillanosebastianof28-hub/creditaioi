12 CFR Part 1006 - Fair Debt Collection Practices Act (Regulation F) Regulation F implements the Fair Debt Collection Practices Act (FDCPA), prescribing Federal rules governing the activities of debt collectors, as that term is defined in the FDCPA. The debt to be collected includes: Consumer Credit - Open-end credit - Closed-end credit - The regulation covers topics such as: Communications in connection with debt collection - Prohibitions on certain conduct in connection with debt collection - Validation information - Time-barred debts - Debt disputes - State exemption programs - Record retention -
