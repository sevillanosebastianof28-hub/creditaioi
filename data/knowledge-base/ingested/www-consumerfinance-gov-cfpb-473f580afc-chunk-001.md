Title XII Banks and Banking Title XII Banks and Banking is one of the titles in the United States Code of Federal Regulations (CFR). The regulations implemented by the Bureau are housed in Chapter X of Title XII of the CFR. Read an introduction to regulations for more background on what regulations are and how they fit into the legal landscape of the United States.
