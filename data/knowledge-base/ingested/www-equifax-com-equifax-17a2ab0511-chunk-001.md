How do I qualify for an additional free credit report? You're unemployed and intend to apply for employment within 60 days. - You're receiving public welfare assistance. - You believe your credit report contains inaccurate information due to fraud. - You've been denied credit or insurance within the past 60 days. - You've placed a fraud alert on your credit reports. - Your state offers a free or reduced-price credit report. - Looking for something else?
