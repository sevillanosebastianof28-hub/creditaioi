Plain writing The CFPB has adopted plain language as a core principle for all consumer-facing content. That means we apply plain language principles in all of our consumer materials. We follow plain language guidelines when creating materials that: Provide information to help consumers make financial choices to meet their own life goals - Provide information to consumers about their rights under the federal consumer laws - Inform consumers about the CFPB activities - Our work Plain Writing Act As outlined in the Plain Writing Act , the CFPB is committed to using plain language to ensure that our publications are accessible to the public.
