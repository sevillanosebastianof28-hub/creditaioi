FY 2024 Annual Performance Report The FY 2024 Annual Performance Report summarizes the Consumer Financial Protection Bureau’s (CFPB’s) progress in implementing the strategies and achieving the goals and objectives developed under former Director Rohit Chopra, as described in the CFPB’s Strategic Plan for FY 2022-2026, for the period of October 1, 2023 - September 30, 2024.
