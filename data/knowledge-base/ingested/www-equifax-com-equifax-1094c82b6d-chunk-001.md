Follow Us For Everyone Equifax Inc. Social media accounts that provide news and information about and from Equifax - who’s who, how business is going and what’s new. Content will range from updates on corporate announcements and business results to snapshots of our corporate culture, employee spotlights, and Equifax in the news. For Businesses Equifax Insights Social media accounts for business customers who are interested in making more informed financial business decisions. Explore these social pages for industry insights, products, and capabilities designed to help our business customers drive smarter and faster decisions. For Individuals Personal Credit These social media pages provide a steady stream of tips and easy to read credit, personal finance and identity theft information to help people make smarter decisions. For those new to credit to established borrowers, these pages help people understand and stay on top of their credit. Our Customer Care team can be found at @AskEquifax.
