The FTC produces a number of reports that examine antitrust and consumer protection trends. The agency uses this research to inform policy and to evaluate the agency’s performance. FTC policy reports include: staff and commission reports about specific topics of interest - economic analyses and reports - competition reports - financial reports - annual reports and other reports about the agency’s activities - As part of its engagement with the international community, the FTC also submits reports to the Organization for Economic Cooperation and Development (OECD) and other international forums.
