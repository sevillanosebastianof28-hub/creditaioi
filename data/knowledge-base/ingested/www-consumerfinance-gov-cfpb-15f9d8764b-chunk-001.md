Rules of Practice for Adjudication Proceedings The Rules of Practice for Adjudication Proceedings govern adjudication proceedings conducted by the Consumer Financial Protection Bureau (CFPB). This final rule rescinds certain amendments it adopted to the Rules of Practice on February 22, 2022, and March 29, 2023 (2022 and 2023 amendments).
