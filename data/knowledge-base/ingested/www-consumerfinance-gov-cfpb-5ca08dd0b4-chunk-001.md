12 CFR Part 1012 - Special Rules of Practice (Regulation L) Regulation L includes special rules of practice relating to filing assistance and adjudicatory proceedings under the Interstate Land Sales Full Disclosure Act. Persons subject to the regulation include: Developers - Agents of developers - The regulation covers topics such as: Pre-filing assistance - Processing of filings - Suspension notices and orders - Notices of proceedings - Hearings - Filing assistance - Adjudicatory proceedings - Additional resources 2015 Bulletin on Interstate Land Sales Full Disclosure Act amendment 2011 Bulletin on communications with CFPB Press release on a May 2015 Enforcement action Guidelines for Exemptions Available Under the Interstate Land Sales Full Disclosure Act
